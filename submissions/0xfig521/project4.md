## Description / 项目简介

zkLogin Paymaster Kit enables gasless transactions on Sui using zkLogin and sponsored transactions. It combines zkLogin authentication with a paymaster pattern to eliminate the need for users to hold SUI tokens for gas fees.

zkLogin Paymaster Kit 实现基于 zkLogin 的无 Gas 交易。将 zkLogin 认证与 Paymaster 模式结合，用户无需持有 SUI 代币即可支付 Gas。
